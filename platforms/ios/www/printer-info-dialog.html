Motor Controls

From this app, you can control the movement of the print head assembly of your printer as well as the height of the print bed.

Your printer has three axes of control: X, Y (both gantry controls) and Z (moving the print bed).

  1. Choose the axis you want to control (X, Y or Z)
  2. Choose the jog amount (this controls how far the print head or bed moves each time)
  3. Use the left/right arrow buttons to move the print head or the up/down arrow buttons to move the print bed
  4. Use the home button to return the print head and print bed to their home positions
