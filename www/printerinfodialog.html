Motor Controls

From this section, you can control the movement of the print head assembly as well as the height of the print bed. Finally, the extruder motor may also be controlled.

Your printer has three axes of control: X, Y (gantry controls of the upper assembly) and Z (moving the print bed platform)...

...as well as the controls for the fourth stepper motor: Extruder (extruding and retracting filament).

  1. Choose the axis/stepper you want to control (X, Y, Z, Extruder)
  2. Choose the jog amount (this controls how far the print head/bed/filament moves each time)
  3. Use the left/right, up/down, extrude/retract pairs of buttons to step the appropriate motor by the indicated amount
  4. Use the home button to return the print head and print bed to their home positions
